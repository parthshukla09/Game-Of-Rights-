Summary of Article 51A

  • Respect the Constitution, the national flag, and the national anthem.
  • Promote harmony and the spirit of common brotherhood among all Indians.
  • Protect the environment, including forests, lakes, rivers, and wildlife.
  • Safeguard public property.
  • Strive for excellence in all spheres of individual and collective activity.
  • Uphold the sovereignty, unity, and integrity of India.
