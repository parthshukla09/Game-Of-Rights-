Article 238: Application to Certain States

  • Application to States:
    • Scope: Article 238 addresses the application of certain provisions related to the judiciary to states with special administrative arrangements. This includes states with unique legal or administrative frameworks.
    • Adaptation: The article allows for the adaptation of judicial provisions to fit the specific needs and conditions of these states.
  • Purpose and Impact:
    • Regional Adaptation: By allowing for the adaptation of judicial provisions, the article supports the effective functioning of the judiciary in states with special administrative arrangements.
    • Legal Consistency: The provision ensures that judicial processes remain consistent with the unique needs and conditions of specific states.
    • Flexibility in Application: The article provides flexibility in applying judicial provisions, helping to address regional variations and administrative challenges.
